FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Zoho One and its suite of apps.

Yes, you can upgrade from Zoho CRM to Zoho One by adding other Zoho One apps that are included in the suite. Zoho One offers a broader range of applications beyond Zoho CRM, like HR tools, finance apps, and project management, all within a single platform.

Yes, you can migrate from any Zoho application to Zoho One. Transitioning gives you access to over 50 integrated apps under Zoho One, streamlining your business functions with a single sign-in (Zoho One login).

Zoho One is a more cost-effective solution compared to Dynamics 365, offering a wider variety of tools for a lower price. While Dynamics 365 is a powerful ERP, Zoho One provides an all-in-one solution that includes CRM, marketing, HR, and more, with flexible pricing options.

Zoho One is a more comprehensive solution than G Suite, as it includes over 50 apps like Zoho CRM, HR, finance, and project management, whereas G Suite focuses mainly on communication and productivity tools like Gmail and Docs.

You can manage multiple organizations under a single Zoho One account, with each company having its own set of users, billing, and admin settings.

To delete a user from Zoho One, go to the Admin Console, select “Users”, and remove the user by choosing the delete option next to their profile.

Zoho One extensions are additional tools and integrations you can add to extend the functionality of the core Zoho One apps, such as integrating third-party applications or adding custom features to existing Zoho apps.

Zoho One offers a more affordable, all-in-one solution for small and medium-sized businesses, while NetSuite is a robust ERP system typically suited for larger enterprises with complex needs. Zoho One includes a wider range of apps for CRM, HR, marketing, and finance, making it more versatile for various business operations.

Yes, Zoho Creator is included in Zoho One, allowing businesses to create custom applications and automate workflows without needing extensive coding knowledge.

Yes, Zoho Flow is included in Zoho One, allowing you to automate workflows across multiple applications, ensuring smooth data transfer between apps.

Yes, Zoho FSM (Field Service Management) is included in Zoho One, enabling businesses to manage field services, including work orders, dispatching, and tracking.

Zoho Landing Page is not a standalone application but is available within Zoho One through Zoho Sites, which lets you build landing pages as part of your website creation process.

While Zoho One provides many functions of an ERP, such as inventory management, CRM, and project management, it’s not a traditional ERP system like NetSuite. It is a more flexible and affordable all-in-one business platform that includes ERP-like features.
